About This Project

Bloom & Share is a heartfelt digital initiative designed to spread love, positivity, and care through the language of flowers. This platform provides a simple yet powerful way to convey meaningful messages using the timeless beauty of flowers. The project leverages the emotional power of flowers—each with its own meaning and significance—to foster deeper connections between individuals. The flower selection process empowers users to browse through 21 unique Sri Lankan flowers, each carrying its own significance and symbolism.
